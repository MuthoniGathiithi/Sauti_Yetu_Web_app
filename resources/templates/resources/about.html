About SautiYetu

Breaking the silence on gender-based violence in Kenya through technology, community partnership, and unwavering commitment to survivor safety and dignity.

Our Mission

To create a safe, anonymous, and culturally appropriate platform that empowers Kenyan women and girls to report gender-based violence, access immediate support, and connect with trusted community resources while maintaining complete privacy and dignity.

Our Vision

A Kenya where every woman and girl can live free from violence, where silence is broken, voices are heard, and communities unite to create lasting change through transparency, support, and collective action.

United in Purpose

Building a safer Kenya together, one voice at a time

Our Goals

1

Create Safe Anonymous Reporting

Design and launch a platform that allows victims to report abuse without revealing their identity, facing no fear of exposure or judgment.

2

Break the Silence

Provide a culturally appropriate, easy-to-use outlet for women and girls — especially in rural and underrepresented areas — to speak out.

3

Connect to Practical Help

Offer curated, localized guidance including immediate steps, NGO contacts, clinic information, and legal rights explained simply.

4

Partner with Community Groups

Build a network of NGOs, churches, youth leaders, clinics, and chamas to promote the platform and offer follow-up support.

5

Prioritize Privacy & Security

Build trust by ensuring no forced registration, no tracking of IP or location, and complete user control over shared information.

6

Enable Community Response

Use anonymous data analytics to reveal patterns and help NGOs and government target support programs more effectively.

7

Make Platform Inclusive

Include voice-recording options, icons, and Swahili instructions to help women who struggle with reading or using English.

8

Scale Responsibly

Begin with a small, local launch, gather feedback from real users, and improve the tool before expanding nationally.

9

Build Community Trust

Involve local women leaders, survivors, and helpers in shaping the platform to ensure it's truly their solution.

How SautiYetu Works

Anonymous & Secure

Our Commitment to Transparency

Built with Community Co-Ownership

SautiYetu isn't just a tech solution—it's a community-owned platform shaped by the voices of survivors, local women leaders, and trusted organizations. We believe in transparency, accountability, and putting survivors first in every decision we make.

Our approach prioritizes dignity, consent, and cultural sensitivity while leveraging technology to break down barriers that have historically silenced survivors of gender-based violence.

Leadership & Unity
