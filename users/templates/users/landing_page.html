Our Mission

About SautiYetu

SautiYetu means "Our Voice" in Kiswahili. We believe every survivor deserves to be heard, supported, and empowered.

Founded with the vision of creating a safe space for survivors of gender-based violence in Kenya, SautiYetu combines technology with compassionate support to break the barriers that prevent people from seeking help.

Our platform is built by survivors, for survivors, with input from leading NGOs, counselors, and legal experts across Kenya. We understand that seeking help can be scary, which is why we've made anonymity and privacy our top priorities.

Privacy First

Your anonymity is sacred to us. We use advanced security measures to ensure your identity remains protected.

Compassionate Care

Every interaction is handled with empathy, understanding, and respect for your journey and choices.

Community Driven

Built by and for the Kenyan community, with culturally sensitive support and local resource connections.
