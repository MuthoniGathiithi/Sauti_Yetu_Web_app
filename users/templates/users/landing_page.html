Breaking the Silence

Gender-based violence affects millions of women and girls across Kenya. Many suffer in silence, trapped by fear, shame, and lack of safe reporting mechanisms.

The Problem We're Solving

  • Fear of Retaliation: Many victims are afraid to report due to threats from perpetrators
  • Social Stigma: Cultural barriers prevent open discussion about abuse
  • Lack of Trust: Distrust in existing systems and fear of not being believed
  • Limited Access: Rural areas often lack proper reporting mechanisms
  • Economic Dependence: Financial reliance on abusers creates additional barriers

Our Solution

SautiYetu bridges the gap between victims and help through technology, anonymity, and verified support networks.

Complete Anonymity

Report incidents without revealing your identity. No personal information required - not your name, phone number, or location. Your privacy is completely protected.

Professional Network

Connected to trained counselors, legal experts, NGOs, and support organizations across Kenya. Get help from verified professionals who understand your situation.

Healing Support

Access self-help resources, support groups, and professional counseling. Start your healing journey with guidance from experienced survivors and experts.

Platform Features

Every feature is designed with your safety and anonymity as the top priority.

Anonymous Reporting

Submit detailed reports about incidents without revealing your identity. Our secure form ensures your story is heard while keeping you safe.

Voice Recording

Can't type your story? Record it instead. Voice messages are encrypted and processed securely by our trained support team.

Bilingual Interface

Full support in English and Swahili with clear navigation and simple language. No barrier should prevent you from getting help.

Resource Library

Access legal information, safety planning guides, and self-help resources. All content is reviewed by experts and updated regularly.

No Tracking

We don't track your IP address, location, or any personal data. Your visit to our platform is completely private and secure.

Community Support

Connect with support groups and other survivors (anonymously). Share experiences and find strength in community.

How It Works

Getting help through SautiYetu is simple, safe, and completely anonymous.

1

Access Platform

Visit SautiYetu from any device. No registration or login required. Your privacy is protected from the moment you arrive.

2

Select English or Swahili. The entire platform adapts to your preferred language for better understanding.

3

Share Your Story

Fill out the secure form or record a voice message. Share as much or as little as you're comfortable with.

4

Get Support

Access resources immediately or choose to receive follow-up support from our trained counselors and partner organizations.
